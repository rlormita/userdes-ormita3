Activity #4





Needfindings: UFUNDME

Problem:

• Society are not informed about the situations in foster homes, that makes foster homes neglected.

• Due to lack of support, some foster children’s mindset may affect that can lead them into bad situations in the future.

• Foster child needs to have a support not only financially but also their necessary needs (education, clothing, spiritual support).

• People are too occupied in their own lives, that hinders them from helping/donating foster homes.

Needs/Objectives:

• A foster home needs to be supported in order to provide the foster child’s needs.

• To allow any people to donate and give support to the foster home easily.

• A foster child needs to know that there is someone that care for them.

• Encourages the people to help not only with money but also the physical needs of the foster children.

Solution:

• Create an application in which anyone can donate and support the foster children’s basic necessities.

It is a crowdfunding application in which the users can donate and provide the ff:

Money – in which can support the foster children financially. The user can donate through their bank accounts and mobile banking (PayMaya/GCash).

Donate a clothing – this is an option if the user does not want to donate money. The user will be given a contact where will be the clothing to be pick-up. A team will go distribute the donation to the chosen foster homes.

• In order to donate, accounts can be created connected to their personal email. If the user wants to donate anonymously, there will be a visitor feature for them.

• The progress of the application will be shown of how many foster homes it reaches.
